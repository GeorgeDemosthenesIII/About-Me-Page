Android: Netrunner

Android: Netrunner is an asymmetrical "living card game" from Fantasy Flight Games. Players take on the roles of a runner and a corporation. The corp player wins by completing seven points worth of "agendas" (evil schemes). The runner wins by stealing 7 points before that can happen (and before the corp finds and kills them). The corp protects their assets and agendas with virtual defenses, which the runner must face each time they wish to intrude.
